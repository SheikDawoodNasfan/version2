Amazon Tea

Amazon is a largest tea manufacturer in Sri Lanka where their brand “English Tea Shop” is marketed and sold more the 50 countries worldwide. At Amazon, they create values by helping to fight poverty and helping the Sri Lankan economy to develop in a sustainable way. Amazon believes the community growth will lead to a growth for themselves.

Production Process

  1. Mountain tea leaves are plucked
  2. The leaves are sorted according to sizes and many other categories
  3. The leaves are then softened to add the flavours
  4. Leaves are rolled to mix it with enzymes in the air
  5. The oxidization process takes place
  6. Finally heated and packed

Venture Tea

Venture Tea is a popular well known company for their tea products. Venture Tea started off in 2005 with average number of employees, and their growth every year has shown a biggest upward trend, and now one the leading Tea manufacturers and exporters of Sri Lanka. Venture Teas is one of the biggest manufacturer and exporter of tea, reaching almost 45 countries worldwide and exporting nearly 5 million kilos of tea every year. Venture Teas are ranked under top 20 for being one the biggest tea exporters of Sri Lanka.

Production Process

  1. Fresh mountain leaves are plucked by hand by experienced tea leaves pluckers
  2. They are checked carefully and taken for the withering process where the leaves become more soften and moisture is taken out
  3. Next is the rolling process, where the leaves are twisted, rolled and processed breaking the cells of the leaves
  4. The flavour is developed and the oxidization process begins
  5. Finally, the leaves are heated, fired and sifted to eliminate the fibre particles

Dilmah Tea

Dilmah is Sri Lankan tea company, also known as the world’s most experienced tea maker. The products of Dilmah since 1988 are now available across the world serving nearly to 100+ countries. Dilmah also been identified as 6th largest tea brand in the world in 2009. Dilmah has a wide range of tea products such as; Ceylon Tea, Flavoured Tea, Green Tea and many more.

History

Dilmah was founded by Merril Fernando who was born in 1930. Merril Fernando trained in London and later in 1974 he purchased tea estates and he produced Sri Lankan tea. Later Merril Fernando talked to an Australian supermarket retailer and requested him to stock his Dilmah tea, and thereafter it became a huge success making all other Australian supermarkets to stock Dilmah tea. Later on Dilmah was exported to New Zealand and since then till now, Dilmah is the best tea selling brand.

Production Process

  1. Fresh tea leaves are hand-picked
  2. The leaves are sorted and cleaned
  3. The withering process takes place
  4. The leaves are then rolled
